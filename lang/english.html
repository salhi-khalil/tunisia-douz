Festival International du Sahara de Douz: a getaway in the sun in the middle of winter

From December 20 to 23, 2022 let yourself be transported by the magic of the Sahara on the occasion of the 51st edition of the Douz International Sahara Festival.

Since 1910 the festival has celebrated the rich Arab-Berber heritage of the region. Douz is the gateway to the Sahara. It is through it that many cultures and generations of nomads and conquerors have passed.

The Mrazigs tribe will welcome you with a rich and varied program that will allow you to better appreciate the different facets of Bedouin life. It is on a background made up of sand dunes that you will discover the traditions of the region; from traditional weddings to mehari races and camel fights.

Festival-goers will have the opportunity to discover local art as rich as it is captivating with exhibitions, music performances and a book fair and documentary screenings.

The freshness of winter will contrast with the gentle warmth of the sun that shines on the emblematic tree of the region: the date palm. During the four days of the festival, the International Festival of Douz invites you to discover the habits and customs of the Berber and Arab cultures.

The festival agenda will appeal to all tastes; lovers of arts, culture, music, sports, history, nature and even those looking for a haven of peace to meditate and enjoy majestic landscapes.

Fly to Douz and lose yourself between its palm trees; fill up on the sun in winter and celebrate the Berber and Nomadic culture with the Festival International du Sahara de Douz!
